Stranger Things Season 5: Cast

Nell Fisher, Jake Connelly, and Alex Breaux are joining the cast of Stranger Things 5. Linda Hamilton (The Terminator, Children of the Corn, Chuck) is also set to join the final season, though details about her role are as mysterious as the Upside Down. But we do know that she’s a major fan of the series. “I don’t know how to be a fangirl and an actress at the same time,” she said. “I’m gonna work on that.”

The full Stranger Things 5 ensemble includes:

Main

  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • Millie Bobby Brows as Eleven
  • Noah Schnapp as Will Byers
  • Gaten Materazzo as Dustin Henderson
  • Caleb Macluaghan as Lucas Sinclair
  • Natalie Dyer as Nancy Wheeler
  • Charlie Heaton as Jonathan Byers
  • Joe Keary as Steve Henderson
  • Sadie Sink as Max Mayfield
  • Maya Hawke as Robin Buckley
  • Jamie Campbell Bower as Vecna
  • Bret Gelman as Murray Bauman
  • Priah Ferguson as Erica Sinclair

Also Starring

  • Cara Buono as Karen Wheeler
  • Nell Fisher as Holly Wheeler
  • Jake Connelly as Derek
  • Alex Breaux as Lt. Akers
  • Linda Hamilton as Dr. Kay
